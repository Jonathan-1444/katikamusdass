Academic Programs

Excellence in education with a Christian foundation

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Our O-Level program follows the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) curriculum, preparing students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations. We offer a comprehensive education that balances academic rigor with spiritual and character development.

Core Subjects

English Language

Developing reading, writing, listening and speaking skills for effective communication.

Mathematics

Building strong foundations in algebra, geometry, statistics and problem-solving.

Geography

Exploring physical and human geography with field trips and practical studies.

Physics

Hands-on experiments and theoretical understanding of physical principles.

Elective Subjects

Biology

Study of living organisms with practical laboratory sessions and field studies.

Chemistry

Understanding matter and its transformations through experiments and theory.

History

Exploring Uganda's and world history with critical analysis of events.

Commerce

Introduction to business concepts, trade, and financial literacy.

Admission Requirements

Minimum aggregate score of 12 in PLE with at least a pass in English and Mathematics.

Students should be between 12-14 years old at the time of admission to S1.

All prospective students and parents/guardians must attend an admission interview.

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

Our A-Level program prepares students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. Students specialize in one of three combinations, receiving in-depth knowledge and skills for university education and beyond.

Combinations Offered

Science (PCM/B)

Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology

Prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, technology, and pure sciences.

Arts (HED/G)

History, Economics, Divinity/Geography

Ideal for students interested in law, arts, social sciences, and religious studies.

Commerce (BAM/ICT)

Business, Accounts, Mathematics/ICT

Prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and management.

Admission Requirements

Minimum of 8 credits including English and Mathematics for Science and Commerce combinations. 6 credits for Arts combination.

Science combination requires credit passes in Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics at O-Level. Commerce requires credits in Mathematics and English.

All prospective A-Level students must pass an interview and placement test in their chosen subject combination.

Vocational Programs

Our vocational programs provide practical skills training alongside academic education, preparing students for both employment and further technical education.

Programs Offered

Computer Studies

Hardware, software, networking and programming

Prepares students for careers in IT support, software development, and digital entrepreneurship.

Building & Construction

Carpentry, masonry, plumbing and electrical

Hands-on training in construction skills for the growing building industry.

Tailoring & Fashion

Garment construction, design and entrepreneurship

Develops skills in clothing production and fashion business management.

Admission Requirements

Completed S4 with at least 4 passes in UCE or equivalent qualification.
